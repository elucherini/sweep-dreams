Privacy Policy

Last updated January 3, 2025

Sweep Dreams is developed by Eli Lucherini, a developer based in California.

Information We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide the app's core functionality:

Location Data
Your device's GPS location is used to find the closest sweeping schedules and provide location-based alerts. Location data is processed in real-time and is not stored. It may temporarily appear in system logs as part of normal operations.

Device Identifier
If you enable push notifications, we store a device identifier to deliver notifications to your device. This identifier is retained only while notifications are enabled. When you disable notifications through the app, your device identifier is deleted from our database. Traces may persist briefly in system logs as part of normal operations.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or any other personal information beyond what is described above. We do not use analytics or tracking services. We do not serve advertisements.

How We Use Your Information

Location data and device identifiers are used solely to provide the app's features. We do not sell, share, or use your information for any other purpose.

Data Retention

  • Location: Not stored (real-time processing only)
  • Device identifier: Deleted when you disable notifications
  • Logs: Retained for a short period for troubleshooting, then automatically purged

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications. See Firebase's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Mapbox to display maps. See Mapbox's Privacy Policy for more information.

Your Choices

You can disable notifications at any time through the app's settings, which will delete your device identifier from our systems. You can also revoke location permissions through your device's system settings.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Contact me at sfsweepdreams@gmail.com.
